The story of my bottom.


Hi there,

I was working as a writer for the first time. Some days I was in a flow state; words of beauty flew out and came together effortlessly.

Then some days were like this. Ideas were slowly coming in, but none were helpful. Some call it Writer's block, But that's simplistic. There must be something else going on.

I lacked creativity, an essential part of a clear, focused mind.
