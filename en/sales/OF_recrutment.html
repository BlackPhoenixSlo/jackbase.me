FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need professional photos and images?

Absolutely not! We know the platform and what works best. Therefore, we can say that it doesn't have to be professional photos and videos. We show our models exactly what they need to do to have the most success.

What if I don't want people from certain countries to watch my content?

In this case, you can use IP address blocking from countries from which you do not want people to be able to search for your account.

What if I don't want to take exclusive photos?

This is an important consideration that everyone must answer for themselves. Our models should only ever go as far as they feel comfortable. Already quite normal and not too excIusive photos are enough.

What reward does the agency receive for providing the service?

We will be happy to discuss the reward question with you by phone after you fill in the form. Our assistant will contact you and make an appointment. You need to take into account that we do all the marketing, have employees that take care of your account and of course are available 24/7 to answer any questions.

I am interested! What happens now?

We are happy to hear that! You now have the opportunity to sign up for a free interview call by phone or via Zoom, which serves the purpose of answering your questions and first of all to get to know us better.


Of course, we don't work with every model, so during the conversation we will look at what your goals and ideas are and whether then a collaboration makes sense at all.


We look forward to hearing from you!
