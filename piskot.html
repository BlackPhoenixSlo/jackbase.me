👨‍🍳 Instructions

1. Process the Base Blend 1 cup of roasted hazelnuts in a food processor until they break down into a thick paste (2–3 mins).
2. Mix Dry & Wet Add oat flour (blended oats), protein powder, cocoa, sea salt, vanilla, and maple syrup to the processor. Pulse to form a dough. Add a splash of water if needed.
3. Form Truffles Flatten a spoonful of dough, place a whole hazelnut in the center, and roll into a smooth ball.
4. Freeze Place truffles in the freezer for 15–20 minutes to firm up before coating.
5. Prep Coating Melt the dark chocolate. Rough chop the remaining 1/2 cup of hazelnuts and fold them into the melted chocolate.
6. Dip and Set Dip each truffle into the chocolate-hazelnut mix, place on parchment paper, and refrigerate until set.

👨‍🍳 Navodila za pripravo

1. Priprava osnove V sekljalniku mešajte 1 skodelico praženih lešnikov 2-3 minute, dokler ne nastane gosta pasta.
2. Mešanje sestavin V sekljalnik dodajte ovseno moko (zmlete kosmiče), beljakovine, kakav, sol, vanilijo in javorjev sirup. Premešajte, da nastane testo. Po potrebi dodajte kapljico vode.
3. Oblikovanje kroglic Vzemite žlico mase, jo sploščite, na sredino položite cel lešnik in oblikujte gladko kroglico.
4. Zamrzovanje Kroglice postavite v zamrzovalnik za 15–20 minut, da se učvrstijo.
5. Priprava preliva Stopite temno čokolado. Preostale lešnike (1/2 skodelice) grobo nasekljajte in jih vmešajte v stopljeno čokolado.
6. Namakanje in hlajenje Vsako kroglico pomočite v čokoladno-lešnikovo mešanico, položite na peki papir in postavite v hladilnik, dokler se čokolada ne strdi.
